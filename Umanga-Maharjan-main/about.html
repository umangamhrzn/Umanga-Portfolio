About
Umanga.

i — Summary In his
own words.

I am a BBA student majoring in Accounting & Finance at Kathmandu University School of Management. The work I gravitate toward sits at the meeting point of finance, business development, and community — the practical mechanics of how institutions reach, serve, and grow with the people around them.

My early professional life has moved between four rooms: the back office of a traditional bank, the front line of an education start-up, the volunteer hall of an international youth organisation, and the courtyard of a Newa cultural ensemble. Each has taught me a different dialect of the same language — financial accountability at Nepal Bank Limited, business development at Young Crafts Academy, leadership and book-keeping at the Rotaract Club of Patan Durbar Square, and rhythm and ritual at Jyapu Samaj Yala.

What I bring to a team is a calm bias toward execution, comfort with numbers, and the kind of patience that produces durable relationships rather than one-off wins. I am especially interested in roles that let me grow inside a finance or business-development function while continuing to work closely with people.
